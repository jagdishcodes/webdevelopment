Monali Dalvi Joshi is an intrinsic & versitile music artist. Monali started her musical career at an early age following footsteps of her father & sister. She completed her formal education with a bachelors degree in Chemistry and MPA vocal (Master of Performing arts) from M.S university baroda. Monali won "Radio city super singer season 2" & "Big FM Golden Voice season 6" and was awarded and endrosed by Sonu Nigam. Monali is widely recognised as an energitic live performer who spans her genre in folk , sufi, classical, semi classical and fusion. Besides being a performer she has extraordinary skill of music composition and writting lyrics.

From the roots of Palanpur - Gujarat, Jigar Joshi is a most popular singer across the region. Jigar is a MPA vocal gold medalist (Master of performing Arts) from M.S university baroda. Jigar appeared as a contestant in "Mati ke laal" by Doordarshan & "Big FM Golden Voice season 3" by BIG FM 92.7. Jigar has even represented Indian music (classical & garba) at countries like UK, Czech Republic and Dubai. He is the one who spans on variety of genres like folk, classical , semi classical , sufi and light music.

With an aspiration of pursuing career in music Richa Barot started her musical journey at an age of 19. Richa completed her BPA & MPA (Bachelors and Masters of Performing Arts) from M.S University. Richa was amongst leading participants in various musical events at state and national level viz "Swarkalash-2015" , "Radio Mirchi Tansen, Vadodara" & "Baroda's Got Talent". She even recorded a playback song for a Gujarati movie. In her early career Richa emerged as a talented performer singer and is widely recognised in musical community in the region. Her key performing areas are in Classical, Semi-classical and light music genres.

Stuti Vora is a dynamic professional vocalist who spans on multiple genres like classical, folk , western and so on. Stuti inherited the art of music from her mother. After her formal education in commercial field she completed Visharad in music and then Masters is performing arts. She won multiple competitions at state level. Professionaly Stuti recorded rhymes for schools and recorded devotional songs for Soham channel. Currently she is a renowned artist in the region.

Sonali Shinde is a popular singer, performer and a dynamic host. She started her musical career at the tender age of 5. She received formal training of music from her father and then from Dr. Janki Mitahiwala. Sonali has performed at various events accross the country and her natural genre is light music, folk and garba. Besides being a commerce graduate and a musical performer sonali has dynamic hosting skills and has hosted number of corporate and social events.
